Set amidst the lush green mountains of the Western Ghats in Kerala, the fabled “God’s Own Country,” about 5 kms past Vagamon, the Falcon Crest is a 3-acre virgin hill-retreat designed to allow guests and residents wanting to get away from the urban rush and enjoy the relaxed mountain fresh environment.

Surrounded by tea gardens, the Falcon Crest is the ideal destination for a peaceful holiday in pristine scenic locales.
The invigorating environment keeps your mind and body in balance while giving you much needed relaxation therapy from the daily rush of city-based living designed to re-charge your spirits and connect with your inner soul.
